About Me

I live in Atlanta, GA, and graduated from the University of Georgia with a BS in Psychology, a BA in Cognitive Science, and a Minor in Anthropology.

I’m interested in the human experience as a whole. Essentially, “Why do people do what they do?”

Since graduating from college, I’ve been trying to figure out what kind of career would allow me to apply the knowledge and skills I learned at UGA, but would also allow me to explore my interest in people. This year, one of my friends mentioned in passing that I might be interested in what she does, UX/UI. I researched the field and realized that UX aligns with my interest in the complex nature of people. We both want to figure out why people do what they do.

I am finishing a certificate in UX/UI at Georgia Tech and as a burgeoning UX designer, I hope to continue exploring why people do what they do in relation to technology.
